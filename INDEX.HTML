About Proofr

Founded by SRCC students, we're revolutionizing early-career hiring by replacing traditional resumes with proof-of-work

Bridging the Gap Between Academia and Industry

Proofr is a pre-seed startup by SRCC undergraduates revolutionizing early-career hiring. We replace traditional resumes with proof-of-work, enabling students to showcase real project experience.

Through curated job boards, portfolio tools, and cohort-based upskilling programs, Proofr connects talent with companies seeking demonstrated skill over pedigree.

Our initiatives include expert-led sessions covering CV building, finance technicals, case solving, and more, conducted by alums from top firms like MBBs and Avendus.
